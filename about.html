Our Story: Driven by Dedication

Rich Land Auto Supply is more than just an automotive business; it's a legacy built on a foundation of quality, reliability, and unwavering commitment to our customers. From our humble beginnings to becoming a cornerstone in Cagayan de Oro City for truck parts and hydraulic solutions, our journey has been fueled by a passion for the automotive industry and a dedication to serving the needs of truck owners and businesses alike.

Conveniently located along Osmena Extension, we've grown into an essential hub for high-end automotive solutions, establishing a solid reputation for excellence and first-rate customer service.
