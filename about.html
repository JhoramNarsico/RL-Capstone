About Rich Land Auto Supply

Rich Land Auto Supply is a respectable automotive business that is well-known for offering quality truck parts and custom-made hydraulic hoses. The business has established a solid reputation for reliability and first-rate customer service, making it a popular choice for both corporations and truck owners.

We are an essential center for customers in need of high-end automotive solutions, conveniently positioned along Osmena Extension in Cagayan de Oro City. Because of its advantageous location, it is easily accessible to both residents and those in the surrounding area, increasing its visibility and allure as a trustworthy supplier of truck parts and services.

Our Mission

To consistently provide our customers with the highest quality truck parts and custom hydraulic hose solutions, backed by unparalleled customer service and technical expertise. We aim to be the leading and most trusted automotive partner in the region.

Our Core Values

  • Reliability: Ensuring our products and services are dependable.
  • Integrity: Conducting business with honesty and transparency.
  • Customer Focus: Putting our customers' needs first.
  • Expertise: Leveraging our knowledge to offer the best solutions.
